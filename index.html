My Habits Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [2023-08-11]

Introduction

Welcome to My Habits! This privacy policy explains how Amal Jose, the developer of My Habits, collects, uses, and stores your data when you use the app. We understand the importance of protecting your privacy and are committed to doing so.

What information does My Habits collect?

My Habits does not collect any personal information about you. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifiable data.

What data does My Habits store?

My Habits only stores app data on your local device. This includes information such as:

  • Habit names and descriptions
  • Habit completion history
  • Streak data for each habit
  • Optional notes you add to your habits

How is data stored?

All data is stored securely on your local device. We do not collect or store any data on our servers or share it with any third parties.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
